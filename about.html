Company Vision & Mission


Panchsheel Engineering works are executing Fabrication and Erection of Radial gate, Barrage gate, Stoplog gate, Sluice gate, Heavy pipe line, heavy structure and Liner under the guidance of well experienced persons worked in public sector undertaking and private enterprises.

Organization H.O. is situated at Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh state with a small workshop at industrial area (Approx. Plot area 23,500 Sq fit). Lathe machine, bending machine and straightening arrangement with handling facility have been installed in the yard.

Since its formation company is attached with M/s KRAM infracon (P) ltd, M/s Jaiprakash Associates Ltd, M/s PEW Engineer’s (P) Ltd, SOMA Enterprise Ltd and later on working as sub-contractor under public sector and well known private enterprises. And we have also registered in Chhattisgarh government.

And we also manufacture & supply Gantry Shutter, Plate Bending Machine, Tee Bending Machine, Electric & Manual winches, Hoist Rope Drum (Electric & Manual) and any other Machines.

Organization is capable of handling field fabrication and erection of steel structures and heavy pipe line work and since its formation experienced in handling Hydro-Mechanical equipment both fabrication and erection works

Present Setup is capable of handling three to four project sites depending up on the project work volume.
