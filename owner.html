Message

S MURGESH

Managing Partner

Panchsheel Engineering Works is one of the highly esteemed engineering group. PEW (Panchsheel Engineering Works) was started in the year 1994 by S MURGESH in Tifra Bilaspur. I have been working to many places across India for our projects. PEW mainly deal with Fabrication and Erection of Radial gate, Barrage gate, Stoplog gate, Sluice gate, Heavy pipe line, heavy structure and Liner. By raising the efficiency, protection and distribution benchmark, the next generation steers further and aims to perpetuate its excellence.

Safety


Panchsheel Engineering Works strives for the SAFEST possible work environment at the job sites and the fabrication shops by continuously improving and evolving safety measures and effectively disseminating safety-related information.

We are committed to provide proper training, tools and equipment to our employees to enable them to perform their duties safely and to aid them in developing proper safety habits. PEW is open to suggestions and contribution from its employees, contractors and partners in its quest for an accident-free work place.
